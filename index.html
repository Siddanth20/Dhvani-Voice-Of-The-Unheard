ABOUT US

Who Are We

Women Empowerment

Dhvani is an N.G.O founded by Prathibha R Rao which helps women develop skill and hand- holds them to earn a living.

We empower women

It Aims at uplifting the lifestyle of Women.

A Cottage Industry

Though Dhvani is an N.G.O it works under a cottage industry module as it believes and wants to be a self-earning organization rather than building up on funds donated.

SERVICES

We Provide Better

A New Skill

It teaches women how to make silk thread jewellery . Then the products are bought back by Dhvani and then sold in the market.

Educates Girls

Dhvani sells its products for profit with a view to educate girl child or train more women to build up a skill.

Light Of Life

Training Sessions

We conduct training sessions for women. If they are interested in making silk thread jewellery they can join us.
